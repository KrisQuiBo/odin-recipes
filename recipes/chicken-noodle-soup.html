CHICKEN NOODLE SOUP

Indulgent and soulful soup

Ingredients

  • 13 c. chicken stock
  • 1/4 c. dry vermouth
  • 1/4 c. butter
  • 1 c. half and half
  • 1/2 lb. (4 c.) med. egg noodles
  • 3/4 c. flour
  • 1 1/4 c. water
  • 2 cups mirepoix
  • 2 c. diced, cooked chicken
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Combine 1 cup of stock, vermouth and butter. Bring to a boil and boil rapidly until mixture is reduced to 1/4 cup (syrupy consistency). Stir in half and half; set aside.
